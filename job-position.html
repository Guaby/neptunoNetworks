Senior Network Engineer/Architect

Neptuno Media seeks a Senior Network Engineer/Architect for work to be conducted in the Guaynabo/San Juan Metropolitan area.

The incumbent must possess at a minimum 5 years of experience and a
bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Electronic Engineering / Computer Science.

The duties of the position include:

- Provides technical expertise and operations support, strategic and tactical planning, and recommendations to management for new technologies and network applications in response to business needs.

- Be a trusted advisor to evolve/grow/optimize existing telecommunication infrastructure and overall return of investment (ROI) of the network.

- Develop Network Implementation Designs and Procedures for installation, deployment, maintenance, and operation duties.

- Evaluate alternative technologies and architectures regarding infrastructure needs.

- Interface with management and vendors to develop and implement new solutions to meet business requirements.

- Design and manage the rollout of critical network infrastructures to support products and services, on both a strategic (long term) and tactical (immediate term) level.

- Perform analytical, technical, cost analysis and CAPEX development work in the planning, design, installation and on-going administration and management of core data, broadband and soft switch (VoIP) networks.

- Ensure that network infrastructure is fully documented and performing as required for internal and customer SLA guarantees and expectations by means of specific metrics and measurements.

- Provide technical expertise and support in network problems and emergencies, analyzing, resolving and documenting such technical problems.

- Provide technical expertise to Product Development to develop new product strategies and enhance existing product sets.

- Interact with telecommunication vendors to obtain pricing and technical specifications for available hardware, software, or services, and also to resolve complex network problems.

- Works with internal partners to evaluate and design solutions for business and customer requirements which will integrate with and enhance the network.

- Create and develop the technical solution and proposal documentation requested for Proposal (RFP) for assigned opportunities.

- Coordinate plan projects and supervise installation of networking hardware/software of telecommunication networks/systems.

- Applies complex design principles and methodology to ensure cost-effective and optimal use of resources and technology without jeopardizing network integrity.

- Provide training and be a mentor for employees in different technical areas.

Special Requirements

- CCIE Service Provider

- Demonstrated in-depth knowledge of LAN/WAN support and engineering
design for voice; data and video networks at Access, Aggregation, and
Core network locations.

- Develop Network Implementation Designs and Procedures for installation, deployment, maintenance, and operation duties.

- Knowledge and experience with TCP/IP data networks using standards
and technologies including but not limited to: MPLS, IS-IS, OSPF, BGP4,
STP, RSTP, 802.1q, 802.1ah, ADSL2+, VDSL2, FTTH

- Knowledge and experience with Cisco routing hardware, software,
protocols, soft switches, Session Border Controllers and firewalls.

- Router, Switch and IP configuration experience is required

If qualified please submit resume to HR Manager of Neptuno Networks at PO Box 191995 San Juan, PR00919
